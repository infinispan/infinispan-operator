= Configuring Service Monitor Target Labels

[role="_abstract"]
You can configure the generated `ServiceMonitor` to propagate Service labels to the underlying metrics via the ServiceMonitor
`spec.targetLabels` field.

.Procedure

. Define labels to apply to your service by setting the `infinispan.org/targetLabels` annotation on your `Infinispan` CR.
. Define a comma-separate list of the labels required in your metrics via the `infinispan.org/serviceMonitorTargetLabels` annotation on your `Infinispan` CR.
